About Us

AOG Logistic S.A.S,a leading company in logistics and transportation of land cargo AOG Logistic S.A.S founded on January 21, 2015 in the city of Bucaramanga with commercial registration No.05-313350-16, enabled by resolution 000021 of March 15, 2015 by the Ministry of Transportation; dedicated to land transportation nationwide

It is directed by its founder Andrés Orejarena Garcia, an enterprising man, passionate about the cargo transportation service and whose initiative and the PERSEVERANCE, COMMITMENT and RESPONSIBILITY of his work team lead him to create a great company with information systems that allow managing operations in real time, and provide advice on processes due to the experience of more than 20 years in the sector.

Mission

AOG Logistic SAS. Logistics solution for cargo transportation nationwide, with high international standards characterized by excellence, quality service and guaranteed corporate values.

Vision

AOG Logistic SAS works to be a leading logistics operator in national and international cargo transportation, generating recognition for its high levels of innovation, technology, flexibility and human quality seeking the satisfaction of our interest groups based on continuous improvement.
